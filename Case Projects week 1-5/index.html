Welcome to Dave's Chess Club

This club is a non-profit organization that has been around since the early 2000's.

Our club welcomes people with no prior knowledge on how to play chess, people who have mediocre at chess, and those who are experienced in playing chess and those who have participated in tournments in the past. This club is a place for those to have fun and enjoy playing chess and making friends.

The following is what we do here at Dave's Chess Club:

  • Inform people what chess is
  • Teach people how to play chess
  • Host pratices and tournments for those who wish to attend
